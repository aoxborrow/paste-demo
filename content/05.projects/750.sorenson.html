Sorenson CMS

HTML/PHP/SQL

Along with the creation of all new sites and branding, Sorenson Communications needed a modern CMS to manage their extensive web presence. I developed a custom platform that allows the marketing team to keep content fresh across their network of sites, including mobile versions. The system is comprised of modular components built with the Kohana MVC framework.

The CMS allows fine-grained control of content and markup with hierarchal navigation, cascading templates and custom page fields. The marketing team deploys from a single development server to multiple QA and Production environments with a simple web interface. All content and assets are version controlled using Git and MySQL. The platform is quite flexible and has been adopted for customer newsletters, internal support documentation and project management.
