Global Leasing Resource

HTML/PHP/SQL

I worked with Contact Design and the creators of World Leasing News to launch Global Leasing Resource (GLR), a new service that aims to revolutionize the leasing industry. GLR provides a directory where leasing companies from all over the world can create a free profile detailing their services. Lenders and service providers can purchase annual subscriptions and access detailed country reports.

The multi-page listing form, subscription tracking and data analysis make for a fairly sophisticated admin interface. A custom CMS was built using MVC principles to keep maintenance easy.
